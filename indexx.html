Mayonnaise Shrimp Recipe

Easy recipe but very delicious like the one in the restaurant!> Alright let's cook

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Prawn 300 gr
Ground ginger 1/2 tsp
Eggwhite 1 egg
Cornstarch 50 gr
Baking soda / baking powder 1/2 tsp
Salt and pepper To taste
Cooking oil Right amount
Mayonnaise 6 tbs
Sweetened condensed milk 2 tbs
Honey 1.5 tbs
Lime juice 2 sdm
Salt 1/4 tsp
Chili sauce and fine garlic (only if you want it spicy) To taste
Green onions, corn vermicelli, black sesame seeds (optional) Right amount

Step 1: Peel the shrimp shells and divide into butterfly chunks.

Step 2: Marinate shrimp with ginger, egg white, 10 grams of cornstarch, baking soda / baking powder, salt and pepper for 15 minutes

Step 3:While waiting for the shrimp to be marinated, mix the dynamite mayo ingredients

Step 4:Heat the oil, then fry the prawns that have been covered with the remaining cornstarch (don't forget to strain it so that there's not too much flour)

Step 5:Serve the fried prawns mixed dynamite mayo. Enjoy your meal :)
